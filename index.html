What I lead

I lead across AI-native product development, design systems and standards, and UX strategy grounded in mixed-method research, finding where AI removes real friction and building it into the flow rather than bolting it on. I treat accessibility as a baseline of product quality, built into the design system and its governance.

And I lead cross-functionally, aligning product, engineering and comms leaders on one problem and one roadmap, while building and mentoring the UX teams that carry the work.

How I work

Starting with where the complexity comes from

I look at product problems beyond the immediate screen, starting with why the problem exists, who is affected, and what constraints the team faces. Often the issue isn't the interface, it's people working around limitations in the product. I bring the recurring issues together, align product and engineering on a practical direction, and stage the work so it ships in phases.
